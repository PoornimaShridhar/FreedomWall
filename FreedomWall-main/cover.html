FREEDOM THE WALL

About Us

Freedom, the Wall is a world-class climbing facility setup by IIT Bombay in collaboration with The Girivihar Club. Established on the 7th of May 2019, it has supported many climbing enthusiasts explore the sport ever since. The club aims towards popularizing the sport and promoting the true spirit of it, among all.


Activities

Weekly Induction sessions

The Freedom community conducts regular Induction cum climbing training sessions, 2-3 times in a week. This is to introduce the participants, in general about the wall and climbing as a sport. Getting inducted is mandatory before any participant climbs the wall, in order to ensure utmost safety at all times.


Sessions are conducted in batches, of size 12-15. Timings : usually between 5-7pm


We also have special sessions on weekends. Timings : mornings/ evenings, based upon instructor's availability


Climbathon

It is a bi-annual event, where in teams can take part in bouldering, lead climbing, speed climbing. Its a fun competition that anyone can try.


Climbing in groups is a great motivation. The competition generally is for the following categories:

1.Men

2.Women

3.Kids


Winners of each category receive excitings gifts.
